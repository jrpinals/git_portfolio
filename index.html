About me

About me

I'm a UX Engineer in training — currently pursuing my MS in User Experience at Arizona State University while building my frontend skills through The Odin Project. My background is in psychology and behavioral health, which shapes how I think about users: with clinical curiosity and a lot of patience.

I care deeply about accessibility and inclusive design. I believe everyone deserves a digital experience that works for them — regardless of ability, age, or tech experience. That belief drives every project I take on.

Before pivoting to UX, I worked as a Registered Behavior Technician, supporting individuals with ASD and developmental disabilities through individualized therapy. That work taught me how to observe, document, adapt — skills that transfer directly to user research.
